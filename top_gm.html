Top Chess Grandmasters

List of Top 5 Grandmasters

  • Magnus Carlsen (Norway)
  • Hikaru Nakamura (USA)
  • Fabiano Caruana (USA)
  • Arjun Erigaisi (India)
  • Praggnanandhaa Rameshbabu (India)

Magnus Carlsen

Magnus Carlsen is widely regarded as one of the greatest chess players of all time. Born in Norway, he became a Grandmaster at the age of 13 and has held the World Chess Champion title since 2013.

Hikaru Nakamura

Hikaru Nakamura, an American Grandmaster, is known for his rapid and blitz chess skills. He is a five-time U.S. Chess Champion and a popular chess streamer.

Fabiano Caruana

Fabiano Caruana is an American Grandmaster who challenged Magnus Carlsen for the World Chess Championship in 2018. He is known for his deep preparation and classical style of play.

Arjun Erigaisi

Arjun Erigaisi is a rising star from India. He became a Grandmaster at the age of 14 and is known for his aggressive and creative playing style.

Praggnanandhaa Rameshbabu

Praggnanandhaa Rameshbabu, also from India, is one of the youngest Grandmasters in history. He has defeated several top players, including Magnus Carlsen, in rapid games.
